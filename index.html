About Us:

Namaste Saathi is a small venture created by some group of school friends with a great motive of creating platform for people who are not getting to use their skills for professional use. It has been started with very less investment and planned to increase the investment as per need. It was started at Dallu Bijeshwori Marga by renting two shutter. We had started our small business from the business of vegetables.


In context of Nepal, we students have experienced drastic change in our life after 10 (SLC/SEE). We students have different creative skills till 10 and we have also got many medals till 10. We are like free bird in the sky till 10. But after 10 due to the drastic change in mentality about the responsibility towards family many of us have to engage with different jobs. Due to this we can not continue our passion. Due to the engagement in both jobs and study we can not do excellency in both what we are capable of doing. We have to spend about 8/10 hours for only just Rs. 7,000 or Rs. 8,000 per month. After some period we could go in depression because of failure in both study and our job. Namaste Saathi targets those who are looking for proper guidance for continuing their passion as their profession. To be clear we have not lots of budget to provide to them but we have our good social network from which we have aimed to provide them necessary social support so that they can continue their passion as their profession also continue their education. We have motive to create good network of skilled youngster. We will be also working as a business consultant for those who have been planning to startup some business but could not start their business because of many confusions.


Sustainability, talking about sustainability we will be also continuing our vegetables business in a professionally managed way.
