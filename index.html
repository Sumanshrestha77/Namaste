About Us:

Namaste Saathi is a venture started by group of friends in Bijayashwori Marga, Kathmandu. Our motive is to find creative and skillful minds within our community and help them to grow, giving them right opportunities where they can apply their creativity and skills, professionally. In addition to it, we are giving training to the youths to enhance their personalities and leadership quality.


For now we have started guitar learning sessions with minimal cost so that interested youth. Furthermore, we are planning to give leadership training the youths soon. We have also started vegetable shop and a cafe to give ourselves base. As, we started this venture with small investment, the vegetable shop and cafe giving us continuity even in this time of covid-19.

Feel free to contact or visit us to know more about us. We are open to any creative ideas and suggestions.
